About Our Company

Your Trusted Partner for Quality Services in UAE

Bustan Altoor Technical Services is a professional technical services company based in Dubai, UAE. With a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we have built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality property improvement and maintenance services.

We provide a wide range of solutions including alternative energy equipment installation and maintenance, floor and wall tiling, carpentry and wood flooring, engraving and ornamentation, wallpaper fixing, false ceiling and light partition installation, and electrical fittings and fixture maintenance.

Our team of skilled professionals uses modern equipment, quality materials, and proven techniques to ensure every project is completed to the highest standards.
